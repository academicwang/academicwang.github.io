Research Data

Curated datasets for empirical research on China's capital markets, corporate disclosures, and labor markets.

Datasets

3

China A-Share Listed Companies — Basic Data

中国A股上市公司基本数据

Comprehensive firm-level dataset covering all A-share listed companies on the Shanghai and Shenzhen stock exchanges. Includes company profiles, financial indicators, corporate governance structures, and shareholding information.

CSMAR / Wind 2000–2024
Data Description & Download

China A-Share Listed Companies — Annual Report Text Data

中国A股上市公司年报文本数据

Full-text corpus of annual reports (MD&A sections) for A-share listed companies. Structured for natural language processing and textual analysis research, including sentiment scoring, topic modeling, and readability metrics.

CNRDS / CSMAR 2007–2024
Data Description & Download

China A-Share Listed Companies — Recruitment Data

中国A股上市公司招聘数据

Job posting data extracted from major Chinese recruitment platforms linked to A-share listed companies. Covers job titles, skill requirements, salary ranges, education levels, and regional distribution for labor market analysis.

Web Crawling / API 2015–2024
