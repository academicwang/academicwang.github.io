Qi Wang

Associate Professor

研究领域聚焦：人工智能与中国经济结构转型。核心探究人工智能等科技创新驱动中国经济结构转型的动力机制，重点剖析具有中国特色的科技金融在人工智能赋能中国经济结构转型过程中的支撑作用与内在机制。

Research focuses on artificial intelligence and China’s economic structural transformation. It investigates the dynamic mechanisms by which technological innovations—particularly artificial intelligence—drive China’s economic structural transformation, and analyzes the supporting role and underlying mechanisms of sci-tech finance with Chinese characteristics in enabling AI to empower China’s economic structural transformation.
